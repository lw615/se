Make sure your writing is clear and concise

Scripta Edit offers editing services to writers and publishers of nonfiction.

Presenting a clear, polished document enhances your professionalism and credibility. Readers can be confused or distracted by wordiness, inconsistencies, grammatical errors, and the like. I can help resolve these issues and ensure your ideas are conveyed effectively. By having your writing professionally edited, you’re making it easier for your audience to focus on what’s important: your message.

With academic degrees in engineering and business, a certificate in copyediting, and extensive professional experience at Silicon Valley companies, I can help perfect many different types of documents. I welcome projects from both native and non-native English speakers.

Contact me at leslie@scriptaedit.com to get started.
