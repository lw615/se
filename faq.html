Frequently Asked Questions

1) How do we get started?
Please contact me at leslie@scriptaedit.com to get started or if you have any questions. I’d love to discuss your project and how we can work together.
2) How much does your service cost?
Each project is unique. The fee will be based on the length of your document, the level of copyediting needed, the complexity of the writing, and your deadline. Once I see your document, I can provide a quote. I bill by the project, not by the hour, so you’ll know the cost upfront and there will be no surprises.
3) How does the process work?
We will communicate throughout the process to make your project successful. Your document and any information you share with me will be kept strictly confidential. The basic steps are as follows.
  1. Email me your project description, document, timeline, and any other relevant information.
  2. I respond with a quote, generally within one business day. I’m happy to provide a free sample edit on a small part of your document if requested.
  3. If you decide to proceed, I send a contract proposal with the scope of work, the schedule, and the fee. As necessary, we work out the details together.
  4. Once the contract is signed, you send the deposit. I edit per our agreed-on schedule.
  5. When the edit is complete, I send you the document with all edits visible in Track Changes (a feature of Microsoft Word). You review the edits and get back to me with your thoughts.
  6. Based on your feedback, I finalize the edits. Then I send the final document, and you make the final payment.
4) Can you guarantee an error-free document?
Because I am human, I cannot guarantee an error-free document, but I will do my best to achieve that. I will strive to make your writing correct, clear, concise, and consistent.
5) What file formats are accepted?
I accept Microsoft Word and use the Track Changes feature so that you’ll be able to see all of my edits. I also accept Microsoft PowerPoint and HTML, but please note that the Track Changes feature is not available in these file formats.
6) What types of payment are accepted?
I accept payment via PayPal, so you can pay with a credit card or bank account.
